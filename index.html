OUR SHOP

Local fresh cuisine

Welcome to Common Grounds Coffeehouse, where people
from all walks of life can enjoy great food, drinks, fun, and
fellowship!
We're a locally owned Coffee Shop with 3 locations in
Lexington. We've worked hard to offer coffee and food
produced and made locally. This is why we now roast our
own coffee weekly. Stop by one of our
stores and enjoy our freshly roasted coffee with a breakfast sandwich or come by
mid-day and try our extensive lunch menu.
